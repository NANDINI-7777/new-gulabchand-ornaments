The Foundation

A Legacy Born of Pure Craft

In 1974, Shri Gulabchand Ji Soni began a small but proud journey in the heart of Jodhpur. With nothing but skill, integrity, and a love for fine gold, he built what would become a name trusted by thousands of families across Rajasthan.

For fifty years, our devotion has remained unchanged. Every hammer strike on raw metal, every custom weld, and every polished ornament carries the legacy of transparency and purity. To us, jewelry is not merely gold or silver; it is an heirloom that keeps memories alive across generations.
